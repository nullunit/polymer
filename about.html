About Polymer

Polymer was founded by two engineers’ love of dessert.

When they took time off to learn how the best dessert was made at scale, they saw firsthand that factory floors were beautiful, messy, and filled with opportunities to make work easier, faster, and more enjoyable.

After countless hours in factories working directly with factory teams, we built the first version of Polymer. Today, we’re building a product to detangle all factory floor operations, so small manufacturers all over the world can build better, faster, and smarter.
